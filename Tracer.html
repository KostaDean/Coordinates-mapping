Interpretive takeaway

These tables isolate the board design itself by removing multiplayer crowding. They answer the right question for publication: not “does a crowded game finish?” but “how does each staging Rim cell behave under the rule system?” This is the proper base for A/B design comparison and for later symbol-removal experiments.
